Euclidean distance (L2 distance)

$$ \sqrt{\sum_i (a_i - b_i)^2} = ||a_i - b_i ||_2 $$

Squared Euclidean distance

$$ \sum_i (a_i - b_i)^2 = ||a_i - b_i ||_2^2 $$

Manhattan distance (L1 distance)

$$ \sum_i |a_i - b_i| = ||a_i - b_i ||_1 $$

Maximum distance

$$ \max _{i}\left|a_{i}-b_{i}\right| $$

Hamming distance

The number of characters that are different between two strings of equal length.

$$ HammingDistance(\text{"hello", "hallo"}) = 1 $$

$$ HammingDistance(\text{"play", "prey"}) = 2 $$

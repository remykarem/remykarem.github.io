## Structure & function

* Epidermis
* Dermis
  * Blood vessels
  * Hair follicles
  * Sweat glands
  * Nerves
  * Connective tissue
* Hypodermis
  * Fat cells
  * Connective tissue

Below the skin is the muscle tissue.

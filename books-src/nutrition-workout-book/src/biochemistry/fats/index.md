# Fats

**Recommended values (per kg of body weight)**
* Minimum: 0.6g[^1] (to avoid hormonal and metabolic issues)
* Calorie deficit: 0.8g-1.0g[^2]

## Food sources

* Others
  * Cocoa butter: 35% stearic, 35% oleic, 25% palmitic

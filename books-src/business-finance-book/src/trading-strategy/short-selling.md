# Short selling

Strategy:
1. Borrow _k_ shares from the broker.
2. Sell the _k_ shares at $q.
3. Wait for market price to drop.
4. Buy back the shares at $p.
5. Return the share to the broker (with interest?).
6. Profit = $q - $p

~~~admonish warning
Short selling can move the market.
~~~

Swift Grader

An iOS mobile app that revolutionises the way teachers mark scripts. This calculator is different from any ordinary calculator as it computes cumulative sum at every button press. It is also designed to minimise any button presses by the use of swipe and longpress gestures.

FAQ

How do I add 0.5 to the button value?

Hold the button and release.

How do I add 10 to the button value?

Swipe right across the blue buttons.

How do I minus to the button values?

Swipe left across the blue buttons.

Privacy Policy

The app only requires user's permission to write to storage files when the user wishes to save the data.

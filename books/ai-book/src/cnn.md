# CNN

## Calculations

- Computational cost
    
    $$
    O(c_{in} \times c_{out} \times k_{size} \times k_{size} \times o_h \times o_w)
    $$
    
- No. of parameters
    
    $$
    c_{in} \times c_{out} \times k_{size} \times k_{size} + c_{out}
    $$
    
    where the last term is for bias

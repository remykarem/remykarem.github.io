On earning your own income

There is virtue in earning a living through one's own labour (as opposed to receiving it).1 2

1

It is better for one of you to gather firewood and carry it on his back thank to ask someone who may give or may refuse. (Sahih Bukhari 1470)

2

No one ever ate any food better than that which he earned with his own hands. And the Prophet of Allah, Dawud, peace be upon him, used to eat from what he earned with his own hand. (Sahih Bukhari 2072)

Business/Finance Book

Wealth

On being wealthy

It's okay to be wealthy1 2.

In fact, being wealthy is a grace3 and opens doors to many opportunities for good deeds3, but that comes with accountability4 5.

If given a choice, being wealthy could be a better option6.

It makes you a strong Muslim7 financially.

On greed for wealth

Having more wealth is usually associated with greed. But if you put your money in your hand, not your heart8, then you have greater control.

On managing finances well

We're encouraged to maintain:

  • a balanced spending9, and
  • entrust our finances to the right people10.

On legacy planning

We're also encouraged to plan the finances for dependents and for our death11.

On growing money

If a lot of money that we have is just getting "taxed", then it might be good to invest it instead.12

Salman Al-farisi divides his wealth into 3 parts, one of it is investments.13
